Target Audience

Description

People who are adults, people who are part of the Chandler Community, people who are leaders in the comunity, people who own businesses in the communnity, people who contribute to the comminuty, people who play a role in the community, people who care about the community, people who have the best interest of themselves and Chandler in mind, people who are proactive or goal driven.

Personas

First Persona:

Name: John

Age: 25

Gender: Male

Education Level: Highschool

Background: John is a highscool drop out who is an aspiring entreprenuer. He started a small business a year ago that has had some success but is now in a rut and having a hard time growing.

Motivation: He is looking to find networking opportunities and meet more like-minded people to give him opportunity to grow his business.

Second Persona:

Name: Kiersten

Age: 40

Gender: Female

Education Level: Bachelors

Background: Kiersten is a lady who is very involved in the community. She has organized many service projects to help in the community, she has hosted multiple community events. She works in a high position in the Chandler School district.

Motivation: She is looking for more ways to become involved in the Chandler community. She always works towards trying to improve the community.
