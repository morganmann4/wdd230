Target Audience

Description

People who are looking for healthy, convienient and fresh food options. These people can be first time customers or customers who regularly order from bountiful foods.

Age: 25

Second Persona:

Name: Kiersten

Age: 40

Gender: Female

Background: Kiersten is in charge of organizing a fun, outdoor music event for her city this year. She has had Bountiful foods in the past and would like for others who come to her event to try their food as well. She is looking at the food options and considering having a Bountiful Foods food truck come to her event.
